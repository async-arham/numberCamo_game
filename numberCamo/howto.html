How to Play

Welcome to the numberCamo Follow these steps to start:

  1. A random number will appear in the Hit section.
  2. Different bubbles with numbers will float across the screen.
  3. Click on the bubble that matches the number shown in the Hit section.
  4. Earn points with each correct hit and aim for the highest score.
  5. The game lasts for 60 seconds, so act fast!

Good luck, and let’s see how many bubbles you can pop!

Timer

You have 60 seconds to hit as many correct bubbles as possible before time runs out.

Score

For each correct bubble hit, your score increases. See if you can beat your best score!

Hit

Pay attention to this number! It’s the number you need to match with the bubbles.
